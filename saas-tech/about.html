Our story

7 years eliminating manual processes in B2B operations

Founded in 2018 by Rafael Mendes and Isabela Costa, Flux Cloud was born out of frustration with automation tools that were too complex for business teams and too limited for engineering. Our mission: democratize intelligent workflows for any team, regardless of technical background.

Over seven years, we have built a platform that combines the power of an enterprise tool with the usability of a consumer product. More than 3,200 companies in 42 countries use Flux Cloud to automate critical operations — from financial approvals to real-time data pipelines.

Today we are a team of 180 people distributed across San Francisco, Lisbon, and New York, backed by tier-1 investors and singularly focused on delivering measurable ROI for every customer within the first 30 days.
