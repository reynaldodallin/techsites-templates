Our story

18 years caring for smiles in Chicago

Founded in 2006 by Dr. Renata Lumina, Lumina Dental was built with a clear mission: to deliver high-quality dentistry with genuine compassion. What started as a solo practice in the heart of Chicago's Magnificent Mile has grown into one of the city's most trusted dental clinics.

Over 18 years, we have built a multidisciplinary team of more than 20 board-certified specialists, served over 12,000 patients, and earned a reputation for excellence in cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, implantology, and endodontics. Every treatment is planned individually, backed by advanced technology and the personalized attention every smile deserves.

Today, Lumina Dental is synonymous with clinical excellence, patient comfort, and life-changing results — one smile at a time.

What Guides Us

Our values and commitments

Every visit to Lumina Dental is guided by principles that place the patient at the center of everything we do.

Compassionate Care

We believe real care begins long before treatment starts. We listen to every patient with attention and empathy, creating a welcoming environment where dental anxiety has no place. Conscious sedation is available for those who need it.

Clinical Excellence

All of our dentists hold post-graduate degrees in their specialties and attend national and international conferences annually. Up-to-date protocols and premium-grade materials in every procedure.

Advanced Technology

3D intraoral scanner, low-radiation digital X-rays, state-of-the-art laser, and digital smile design. Visualize your results before treatment even begins — with a personalized digital preview.
