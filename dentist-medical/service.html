Laser Whitening

Sessions 1 session
Duration 90 minutes

About this treatment

Laser teeth whitening is the most sought-after cosmetic procedure in modern dentistry. At Lumina Dental, we use state-of-the-art laser technology combined with a high-concentration whitening gel, activated by cold LED/laser light — delivering safe, uniform, and long-lasting results in a single session.

The procedure is performed with full protection of the gums and soft tissue, eliminating any risk of excessive sensitivity. Our exclusive post-whitening hydration protocol ensures patient comfort during and after the treatment. Immediate results can achieve up to 8 shades of whitening, completely transforming your smile in under two hours.

Procedure highlights

  • Immediate results — up to 8 shades whiter in 1 session
  • Pain-free procedure — no anesthesia required
  • State-of-the-art LED/laser technology
  • High-concentration whitening gel with gum protection
  • Results last 12 to 24 months with proper maintenance
  • At-home maintenance kit included

Ideal for

  • Stains from coffee, tea, wine, and tobacco
  • Natural enamel darkening over time
  • Patients who want fast results before a big event
  • Pre-treatment before porcelain veneers or smile design
