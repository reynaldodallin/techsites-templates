Our Story

8 years building brands that leave a pulse on the market

Founded in 2018 by Ana Pulse, Pulse Studio was born with a clear conviction: brands that pair bold creativity with data strategy grow faster, reach further, and stay more consistent. In 8 years, we've delivered more than 180 successful cases in branding, performance, and content for clients across the US and beyond.

We are a boutique agency — small by choice, not by limitation. Every client gets real attention from senior specialists, with no intermediaries and no templated deliverables. Our methodology blends market research, design thinking, and performance data to create solutions that actually work.

Today we are a team of 25+ specialists in creative, strategy, media, and content — and proud partners to brands like Verko Tech, Fenix Capital, and Atalaia Fashion.
