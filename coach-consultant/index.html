Our Mission

12 years accelerating high-performance leaders across the globe

Aurum Coach was founded on the conviction that every executive holds untapped potential. Established in 2013 by Rafael Aurum — a former CEO with experience across three continents — the firm has become the benchmark for high-impact executive coaching in North America.

Our proprietary Aurum Method combines applied neuroscience, systemic leadership frameworks, and rigorous outcome accountability. Over 12 years, more than 1,800 professionals from 18 countries have transformed their careers, relationships, and results through our programs.

We work with C-Suite executives, leaders in transition, and entrepreneurs seeking more than advice — they seek real, lasting change.
