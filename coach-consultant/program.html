About this program

Executive Mastery is Aurum Coach's most advanced offering. Designed for CEOs, C-Suite leaders, and executives in high-stakes transitions, it combines deep leadership-profile diagnostics, ontological coaching, applied neuroscience tools, and weekly outcome accountability.

Every engagement is fully customized for the client, anchored by an initial 360-degree assessment that evaluates leadership style, behavioral patterns, limiting beliefs, and untapped potential. Your coach is Rafael Aurum, with over 12 years of experience and 1,800+ executives served.

What is included

  • 12 individual 90-minute sessions
  • 360-degree leadership assessment
  • Individual development plan (IDP)
  • Between-session support via messaging
  • Monthly progress report
  • Exclusive Aurum Method tools
  • Access to the Aurum digital library
  • Closing session with next-steps roadmap

Who this program is for

  • CEOs, CFOs, CMOs, and other C-Suite executives
  • Executives transitioning roles or organizations
  • Founders and high-growth entrepreneurs
  • Leaders who want to scale without losing who they are
