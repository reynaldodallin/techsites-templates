Our Story

18 years defending our clients' interests with legal excellence

Founded in 2007 by Charles Vertex, Vertex Law was built with a clear mission: deliver legal counsel that combines the personalized attention of a boutique practice with the technical depth of a large firm. Over 18 years, we have handled more than 1,200 matters with a proven record in corporate, tax, and contract law.

Our edge is the personalized strategy we develop for every client. Each engagement begins with a rigorous legal analysis — comprehensive risk mapping, document review, and precedent research. We only recommend the path we would advise for someone in our own family.

Today our team includes 30 attorneys and legal consultants, serving growth-stage startups through multinational corporations seeking the highest level of legal protection.
