About this project

Cafe Mirante was born from the dream of two siblings who wanted to turn a privileged vantage point in Brooklyn into a welcoming specialty coffee space. The mission was to create an identity that captured the balance between sophistication and warmth — where the view meets the aroma.

I developed the entire visual identity system around the idea of an observation point: lines that expand, a monogram that suggests movement and height. The palette of warm earth tones with touches of moss green evokes coffee, nature, and the views the place offers. The result is a brand that feels handcrafted without being rustic, contemporary without being cold.

What was delivered

  • Logo in primary, secondary, and symbol versions
  • Complete visual identity guidelines
  • Primary and secondary color palette
  • Typography selection and hierarchy
  • Stationery: business card, letterhead, and envelope
  • Packaging design (bean bags and boxes)
  • Printed menu (A5 front and back)
  • Social media templates (feed + stories)
  • Interior signage (entrance sign and stickers)
  • Final files in AI, PDF, PNG, and SVG

Creative process

  • Brief and deep dive into the client's world
  • Reference research and mood board
  • Hand sketches and 3 distinct creative directions
  • Vectorization and refinement in Adobe Illustrator
  • Presentation with realistic mockups and rationale
  • 2 revision rounds included in scope
  • Full application delivery in 4 weeks
  • 30-day post-delivery support
