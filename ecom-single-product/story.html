Our Story

5 years building the insulated bottle that belonged in your life

Founded in 2021 by Lucas Veiga and Camila Duarte, Nuvo Hydro was born from frustration with bottles that promised performance and delivered disappointment. Both founders, passionate about design and sustainability, spent two years testing materials, insulation technologies, and finishes before launching their first product to market.

The difference is in the engineering. Every Nuvo Hydro bottle is crafted from food-grade 18/8 stainless steel with ThermoLock™ double-wall vacuum insulation, tested between -4°F and 212°F before leaving the production line. The leak-proof lid with food-grade silicone ring underwent 50,000+ open/close cycles in lab testing before approval.

Today we ship across the US and 37 countries, with over 150,000 units sold and one of the lowest return rates in the category: 0.8%. For us, every bottle is a commitment to quality — and every satisfied customer, proof that we're on the right track.

What Drives Us

Quality

Every bottle passes rigorous inspection before it reaches you. Food-grade materials, precision welding, and pressure testing ensure superior performance for years.

Sustainability

One Nuvo Hydro bottle replaces 500+ plastic bottles per year. 100% recyclable packaging, carbon-offset operations, and an end-of-life recycling program.

Design

We believe a water bottle can be as beautiful as it is functional. Every color, curve, and lid detail is the result of hundreds of hours of iterative design focused on the joy of use.
