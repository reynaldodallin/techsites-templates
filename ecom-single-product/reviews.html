About this product

The Nuvo Hydro 17 oz Ocean Blue is built for people who take hydration seriously. Crafted from food-grade 18/8 stainless steel with a scratch- and impact-resistant powder coat finish, it keeps drinks ice-cold for up to 24 hours and hot for up to 12 hours thanks to ThermoLock™ double-wall vacuum technology.

The 17 oz size is the most versatile in the lineup — perfect for work, the gym, or the trail. Ocean Blue delivers a deep, rich color that won't fade, won't transfer flavor, and won't hold odors. The wide-mouth double-thread cap provides a perfect seal and a wide opening that fits ice cubes.

Technical specifications

  • Capacity: 17 oz (500 ml)
  • Weight: 11.3 oz / 320 g (empty)
  • Dimensions: 3" dia × 10.2" tall (7.5 cm × 25 cm)
  • Material: 18/8 food-grade stainless steel
  • Exterior finish: textured powder coat
  • Lid: wide-mouth, double-thread, silicone leak-proof seal
  • 100% BPA-free and toxin-free
  • Standard car cup holder compatible
  • Wide-mouth opening — fits ice cubes
  • No flavor transfer, no odor retention
  • Hand wash recommended
  • Warranty: 2 years against manufacturing defects

Insulation technology

  • Double-wall vacuum (ThermoLock™)
  • Cold drinks stay cold for up to 24 hours
  • Hot drinks stay hot for up to 12 hours
  • Condensation-free exterior wall
  • Food-grade silicone O-ring leak-proof seal
  • Non-slip rubberized base
  • Tested between −4°F and 212°F (−20°C to +100°C)
