About this home

An immaculate penthouse in the heart of East Austin, finished with premium materials, sweeping panoramic views of the downtown skyline and Barton Creek Greenbelt, and ideally positioned minutes from South Congress shops and the best breakfast tacos in the city. Move-in ready with a designer-furnished interior and custom live-oak millwork throughout.

The lower level features an open-plan great room, a powder room, a chef's kitchen integrated with the dining area, and a primary suite with a walk-in closet. Upstairs, three additional bedroom suites, a cozy TV lounge, and access to the rooftop terrace with a heated pool, outdoor kitchen, and landscaped garden under a canopy of Hill Country sky.
