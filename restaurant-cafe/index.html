What we offer

A complete experience

From the oven to the glass, every detail is crafted so your visit to Forno & Co is truly unforgettable.

Wood-Fired Oven

Our pizzas are baked in a Neapolitan wood-fired oven at 480°C. The crust rises to perfection in 90 seconds, thanks to a 48-hour natural fermentation with house levain.

House-Made Pastas

Every pasta is rolled fresh daily in our cucina using imported Italian semolina and local farm eggs. Tagliatelle, pappardelle, and ravioli — made with love.

Wine List

Over 140 labels thoughtfully selected by our sommelier, spanning the finest Italian appellations — Barolo, Brunello, Amarone — alongside standout American bottles.

Private Dining Room

A dedicated space for corporate dinners, birthdays, and celebrations of up to 30 guests, with an exclusive menu and a dedicated sommelier.
